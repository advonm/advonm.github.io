About

I am a biomedical researcher working across neurodevelopment, congenital heart disease, and translational surgical science. My work spans basic mechanistic studies, clinical outcomes research, and preclinical device and tissue-engineering development, with an emphasis on rigor, reproducibility, and clinically meaningful interpretation.
